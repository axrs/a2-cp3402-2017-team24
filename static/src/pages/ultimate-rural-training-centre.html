Ultimate Rural Training Centre

Looking for an opportunity to gain hands-on experience whilst forming your industry skills and knowledge to help start your career on a cattle station?

This is a full qualiﬁcation offering both face-to-face and on-the-job Training in Townsville with Toomby Horsemanship Ultimate Rural Training Centre, quality training provided by local industry qualiﬁed and experienced trainers, in a supported learning environment. This training program is delivered in partnership with LD Training.

Course Overview

This training course is a nationally recognised qualification. The qualiﬁcation reflects the role of employees such as station hands on a cattle station who perform tasks including safe rural procedures. It is an opportunity to gain some hands-on experience whilst forming an understanding of industry expectations. Learning outcomes include training in specialised techniques and procedures that are used in the industry, legal compliance requirements, risk management systems and continuous improvement processes.

This qualification is delivered under a partnership arrangement with LD Training. A Registered Training Organisation, the scope of training approved for delivery by LD Training includes this qualification, AHC32816 Certificate III in Rural Operations.

As a Pre-Qualified Supplier (PQS) of training programs funded by the Department of Education Queensland, LD Training offer training programs funded by the Department's Certificate 3 Guarantee Training, including this course, AHC32816 Certificate III in Rural Operations.

More information about LD Training and the Government's Certificate 3 Guarantee Funding can be found on their website: www.ldtraining.com.au

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age to enrol in this course?

One of the eligibility requirements for Certificate 3 Guarantee training is that you must be at least 15 years of age prior to enrolment. Be sure to read the Certificate 3 Guarantee Pre Enroment Information Sheet to find out more about the eligibility requirements.

What is the maximum age to enrol in this course?

There are no restrictions on how old a person may be to enrol in this course.

What is the minimum age for working in this industry?

Whilst the National Employment Standards identifies the minimum age of employment, we encourage anyone interested in a career within this industry to do their research on working in this industry. Most employers are located in very remote areas, prodominently offering single accommodation, however some worksites can accommodate couples and even families. Living and working on a Cattle Station is suitable to those who possess the skills to live independantly.

Is it ok to do this course if I have never ridden a horse before?

Yes! a number of our past students have no previous experience in riding horses. As part of our training, Team Toomby commences with the basics of horse riding for all students.

I am not a young person at at risk, can I still enrol in this course?

Yes! If you meet the eligibilty requirements for Certificate 3 Guarantee training, you can enrol in this course. In the past, Team Toomby have delivered training programs under funding arrangements where participants must meet specific eligibility requirements. Where future courses are delivered under these arrangements, all promotional material and pre-enrolment checks will include details of this.

Is accommodation included as part of the training program?

Unfortunately the cost of accommodation is not funded under this training program. Single room accommodation only may be available at site for students who are able to live and support themselves independantly, at a reduced cost to students.

Is public transport available to and from course each day?

Toomby Horsemanship Ultimate Rural Training Centre is located approximately 10 minutes out from Willows Shopping Centre. Public Transport is not available to this area. When possible, Toomby Horsemanship will arrange a single pick up and drop off to Willows Shopping Centre each day, at a cost to students.

Will I get a job when I have completed this course?

Toomby Horsemanship are not an employment agency, we delivery training programs in partnership with our RTO, LD Training and are not required to find jobs for students on completion. We will encourage students throughout the course to prepare for and seek out employment opportunities. Your commitment to learning and developing the knowledge required for working in this industry will go a long way to helping you find employment.
Team Toomby has an extensive network of contacts on properties throughout the North Queensland and Northern Territory Region and often become aware of employment opportunities, which they may share with students.
