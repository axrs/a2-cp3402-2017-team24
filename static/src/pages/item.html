The Purpose of a Pad

First and foremost a saddle pad makes a saddle more comfortable for the horse. It helps to eliminate friction between the saddle and the horse’s back. It also provides cushioning for the horse and rider. Wool felt saddle pads help to relieve pressure points and disperse the weight of the saddle and rider evenly. They also reduce slippage due to sweat. There are several pads on the market today and only wool felt has stodd the test of time. To keep your pad lasting even longer, clean your saddle pad often.
